SM Sitara competition

SM Sitara Winner gets, SM Sitara Title, INR 35000/- and many prizes with gifts in StarMaker.

Runner-up gets INR 10000/- and many prizes with gifts in StarMaker.

Second Runner-up gets INR 5000/- and many prizes with gifts in StarMaker.

All the participants gets SM Sitara participation certificate, consolations prizes and many gifts in Starmaker.

In any competition all are winners. However, first, second and third has to be chosen based on some criterias.

Focused Criterias

  • Sur(Notes)
  • Diction
  • Voice quality
  • Personality
  • Appearance
  • How you handle your mistakes
